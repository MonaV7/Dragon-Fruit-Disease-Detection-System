Model Guide

How to Choose the Right Model?

Dragon fruit diseases can affect different parts of the plant. Look at your plant carefully and pick the model based on where and how the problem appears.

🌿
Stem Model
Stem Canker
Anthracnose
Cactus Virus X

Use when you see:

  • ✓ Cracks or splits on the main thick stem
  • ✓ Dark brown or black rotting patches on the stem
  • ✓ White or grey fungal growth on stem surface
💡 Farmer tip: If the problem is on the thick green body of the plant — not the thin branches — use the Stem Model.
Detects: Stem Canker Anthracnose Cactus Virus X
🍂
Spot Model
Brown Spot
Soft Rot
Black Spot

Use when you see:

  • ✓ Small round black, brown, or grey spots on the surface
  • ✓ Wet or dry lesions and patches spreading outward
  • ✓ Yellow halo rings around dark spots
💡 Farmer tip: If you see spots, dots, or patches anywhere on the plant — even if the stem looks fine — use the Spot Model.
Detects: Brown Spot Soft Rot Black Spot Gray Blight Healthy Stem
